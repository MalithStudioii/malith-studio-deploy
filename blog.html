Our File to Text Tool Just Got a Major Upgrade!

Published on September 19, 2025

We're excited to announce a massive update to one of our most popular tools, the Image to Text Converter! It’s now a more powerful and versatile File to Text Converter, with a host of new features designed to streamline your workflow.

So, what's new? Firstly, you can now extract text not just from images, but also directly from PDF documents! But we didn't stop there. After extracting your text, you can now Export it directly to a Word (.doc) file, with special fonts for Sinhala and Tamil to ensure perfect formatting. Plus, with new action buttons, you can send the extracted text directly to our Text Summarizer or AI Writing Assistant with a single click.

This update is all about creating a seamless experience between our tools, saving you time and effort. Check out the upgraded tool now and see how it transforms your document handling tasks!
